# Project context

## Who are we building for? (one paragraph)
Write one short paragraph describing the real people this is for. Include who they are, what they are trying to do, and any important constraints they face.

## What problem are we solving? (one paragraph)
Describe the specific problem in plain language. Focus on what is hard today, who is affected, and why it matters now.

## What does good look like? (two or three sentences)
Describe what success looks like when this works. Be concrete about the outcome you want, not just the feature you want to build.
